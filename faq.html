Frequently Asked Questions

When is the Spring 2021 application deadline?

Our Spring 2021 application closes on February 21st at 11:59 PM PST. We will spend the following week doing final round interviews with our top candidates, before finalizing the cohort on March 2nd.



Who can apply?

We accept current and former Trojan founders/founding teams, at all degree levels.



What characterizes a strong applicant?

WorkBench likes ideas, but we love founders. A strong applicant demonstrates empathy, candor, learnability, and an eagerness to solve the problem at hand.



What stage startups are you looking to compete?

WorkBench is open to applicants of all stages. Whether it’s just an idea, or you are already on the market, WorkBench wants you to apply!



What kind of help can you provide to the startups?

The WorkBench team is multidisciplinary to its core. We have demonstrated leaders in Tech, Finance, Marketing, Design, Operations, and Product Management right at your disposal.



What’s the time commitment?

WorkBench will host a pitch-clinic for the founders every-other week, totalling 5 meetings for the whole semester. Founders, however, are expected to meet additional hours with WorkBench members outside the regular meetings.



Is this all virtual?

Yes.
